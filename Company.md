**Main Objects:**

- To carry on business as developers, designers, programmers, consultants, and providers of software solutions, applications, and IT services across various platforms and technologies.
- To provide telecom solution development services including AAA (Authentication, Authorization, and Accounting) systems, RADIUS servers, billing systems, charging platforms, 3G/4G/5G network solutions, and carrier-grade telecommunications infrastructure.
- To offer solution integration and system integration services including enterprise application integration, API integration, third-party system integration, legacy system modernization, and end-to-end solution architecture design and implementation.
- To provide cloud computing services including cloud infrastructure design, migration, deployment, management, and optimization across public, private, and hybrid cloud environments.
- To offer DevOps consulting and implementation services including continuous integration, continuous deployment (CI/CD), infrastructure automation, containerization, and orchestration solutions.
- To develop, design, and deliver custom software applications, web applications, mobile applications, enterprise solutions, and SaaS-based products for clients across various industries.
- To provide IT consulting services including technology assessment, architecture design, infrastructure planning, digital transformation strategy, and IT optimization services.
- To offer infrastructure automation services including configuration management, automated deployment, monitoring solutions, and infrastructure as code (IaC) implementation.
- To provide managed IT services including application maintenance, cloud infrastructure management, DevOps support, and technical support services on retainer or project basis.
- To conduct training, workshops, and knowledge transfer programs on emerging technologies including cloud platforms, DevOps practices, automation tools, telecom systems, and software development methodologies.
- To develop and commercialize proprietary software products, tools, frameworks, and intellectual property in the areas of telecommunications, cloud computing, DevOps, automation, and IT infrastructure management.
- To establish partnerships and collaborations with technology vendors, telecom operators, cloud service providers, software companies, and system integrators for mutual business growth.

**Ancillary/Incidental Objects:**

- To acquire, develop, maintain, and commercialize software licenses, cloud certifications, technology partnerships, and intellectual property rights related to telecommunications, cloud computing, and IT infrastructure.
- To import and export software products, IT services, telecom solutions, and technology solutions in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
- To provide staff augmentation services, resource outsourcing, and dedicated development team services to clients globally, including specialized telecom engineers and DevOps experts.
- To undertake research and development activities in emerging technologies including artificial intelligence, machine learning, blockchain, IoT, 5G/6G technologies, and other innovative IT and telecom solutions.
- To provide system integration services for telecom operators, enterprises, and service providers including OSS/BSS integration, network management systems, and billing platform integration.
- To enter into contracts, agreements, joint ventures, partnerships, and collaborations with other entities including telecom operators, equipment vendors, and technology companies for business expansion and service delivery.
